2.  Problem  Statement  The  CSE  department  hosts  five  active  student  clubs,  each  running  independently  with  no  
unified
 
communication
 
channel.
 
This
 
results
 
in
 
several
 
recurring
 
problems:
   ●  
 
Students  miss  important  notices  because  they  are  spread  across  multiple  
platforms.
 ●  There  is  no  structured  way  to  distinguish  club  executives  from  regular  
students.
 ●  Events  go  unattended  because  reminders  are  inconsistent  and  unreliable.  ●  Club  executives  have  no  dedicated  tool  to  manage  their  community  posts.  ●  There  is  no  centralized  archive  of  past  club  activities  and  notices.  CSE  Club  Hub  directly  addresses  all  of  these  issues  through  a  single,  purpose-built  
application.
 
3.  Project  Objectives  
1.
     
Build  a  Flutter-based  cross-platform  mobile  app  for  Android  and  iOS.  2.
     
Integrate  all  five  CSE  department  clubs  into  one  unified  platform.  3.
     
Implement  a  role-based  access  system  (Admin,  Executive,  Student).  

--- PAGE 2 ---
4.
     
Enable  real-time  push  notifications  for  club  announcements  and  events.  5.
     
Provide  executives  with  tools  to  post  notices  (text  and  image)  and  manage  events.  6.
     
Allow  students  to  follow  specific  clubs  and  receive  personalized  updates.  7.
     
Make  the  app  deployable  on  the  Google  Play  Store  for  real-world  usage.

5.  Core  Features  

--- PAGE 3 ---
5.1  Authentication  &  Profiles  ●  Email/password  signup  with  student  ID  validation  ●  Profile  setup:  name,  batch,  section,  department,  avatar  ●  Role  assignment  managed  by  Super  Admin  
5.2  Club  Feed  ●  
 
Personalized  home  feed  showing  posts  from  followed  clubs  ●  
 
Department-wide  feed  showing  all  clubs'  posts  ●  
 
Post  creation  with  text  and  image  upload  (executives  only)  ●  
 
Pin  important  posts  to  the  top  of  the  feed  ●  
 
Reactions  (Like,  Fire,  Clap)  and  comment  threads  
5.3  Events  Module  ●  
  
Executives  can  create  events  with  title,  description,  date,  time,  and  venue  ●  
 
Students  can  RSVP  as  Going  or  Interested  ●  Automatic  push  notification  reminder  24  hours  before  the  event  
5.4  Smart  Notifications  ●  Instant  push  notification  when  a  followed  club  posts  a  notice  ●  
 
Event  reminders  sent  automatically  via  Firebase  Cloud  Messaging  ●  Notification  center  with  read/unread  status  
5.5  Search  &  Discovery  ●  Search  posts,  events,  clubs,  and  users  ●  
 
Browse  all  clubs  and  follow/unfollow  with  one  tap
